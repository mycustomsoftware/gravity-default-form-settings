== Description ==
Streamline Your Forms with Gravity Default Form Settings.
Introducing Gravity Default Form Settings, the ultimate solution to enhance and streamline your form creation process within Gravity Forms. This indispensable plugin is tailored for administrators and webmasters who seek consistency and efficiency in their form-building workflow. By allowing users to pre-define default form fields, notifications, and confirmation settings, it ensures that every new form created on your website automatically includes essential fields like name and email, assigned with consistent IDs for seamless shortcode integration across notifications.

With Gravity Default Form Settings, you can:

Set Default Form Fields: Automatically add predefined fields to every new form, saving time and ensuring consistency across your site.
Automate Notifications: Configure default notification settings once, including the admin email and default message, and have them applied to all new forms without additional edits.
Customize Confirmation Settings: Establish default confirmation settings for new forms to streamline user interactions and responses from the outset.
Enhance Workflow Efficiency: Simplify form creation, ensure uniform field IDs for easy shortcode use in notifications, and minimize repetitive setup tasks.
Perfect for any Gravity Forms user looking to minimize repetitive tasks, maintain consistency in form fields and notifications, and enhance the efficiency of their form creation process. Embrace the simplicity and consistency of Gravity Default Form Settings and transform your form management experience today.
